Training Principle: Overload

You MUST challenge yourself every week to build mass. Focus on one of these:

  • **Increase Weight:** Use slightly heavier weight.
  • **Increase Reps:** Do more reps within the 8-12 range.
  • **Time Under Tension:** Slow down the lowering (negative) phase to 3-4 seconds.

    • Your lift tracking automatically uses your last weight as your target for the next session.
